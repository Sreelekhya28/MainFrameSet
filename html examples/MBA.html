MBA DEPARTMENT

Earning an MBA is about preparing for a career as business leaders, managers, entrepreneurs, and consultants. The Department of MBA at VJIT has been able to create some of the best managerial talent for the past decade. Established in 2006 the Department is known for an energetic learning environment and offers specializations in Finance, Marketing, HRM and Entrepreneurship. Case study based approach to learning and broad emphasis on soft skills development ensures that the students gain the confidence to become the most excellent managers. The department vision is to encourage high quality research, and promote excellence in management education through distinctive teaching and learning methodology. The main emphasis is to create a culture of autonomy, for ensuring a high-performance work environment, and to address diverse areas which are of concern to varied sections of society.
